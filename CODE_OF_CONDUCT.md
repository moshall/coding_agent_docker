# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We commit to maintaining a respectful, harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on actionable technical feedback.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Doxxing or publishing private information.
- Repeated disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, reject contributions, or ban participants for violations.
